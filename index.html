Hello, my name is Feiyang Jin, a first year PhD student at Georgia Tech. My advisor is Vivek Sarkar

My research focuses on Parallel Computing. The problems I look at include but not limited to race detection, deadlock-prevention, algorithms, etc. I also have an interest in Robotics. My past experience touches fields of task simulation, communication protocols, sensors and ROS.

I finished my undergradute study at Washington University in St. Louis in Dec 2019. It is my great honor to have worked with two distinguished professors at WUSTL: Prof. Xuan Zhang and Prof. Kunal Agrawal.
